I was born in Reynoldsburg Ohio and graduated from The Ohio State University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Information Sciences.

I am an expert Mainframe programmer mainly in COBOL, CICS, DB/2, JCL, VSAM, and Assembler using TSO/ISPF, Changeman, QMF, Quality Center, Debug, and Fault Analyzer. I am also proficient in XML, IMS, and PL/I.

I moved to Victorian Village long before it was the cool part of town! My work on different non-profit boards in the neighborhood helped to make it the cool part of town too. I was a founding member of the Short North Neighborhood Foundation that raised the money to put Pocket Parks along High Street and the arches over High Street as well. I spent 10 years on the board of the Friends of Goodale Park and a term as their Secretary while we raised the money to get the new fountain built in the pond in Goodale Park. I was the Treasurer and a board member of the Columbus Blues Alliance which brings the Traditional Blues Festival to town every year. I was Secretary of the Victorian Village Society, and Secretary of the Spring-Sandusky Interchange task force that fought for years to lessen the impact of the yet to be built I670 freeway on our Federally Protected Historic Neighborhood too. Currently I am the BeerMiester that runs the after party for the DooDah Parade every 4th of July in the Short North and last year we raised over $10,000 for charity!

Currently, I have one more week of guided instruction in my WeCanCodeIt Java Bootcamp and have a perfect 100% on all of my projects to date. I have become quite skilled at Java, HTML, Java Script, jQuery, CSS, JSON, Spring MVC, H2 Databases, and AJAX through my work with Bootstrap, Eclipse, Sublime, Github, Gitbash and Slack.
